**TABLE 1: public.cricket_one_day_international**
One row per ball bowled in ODI matches.
Columns:
- Identity: match_id, season, start_date, venue, innings
- ball: over.ball identifier like `7.5` (NOT a total count; use `COUNT(*)` for ball totals)
- Teams/players: batting_team, bowling_team, striker, non_striker, bowler
- Outcome: runs_off_bat, extras, wides, noballs, byes, legbyes, penalty
- Dismissal: wicket_type, player_dismissed, other_wicket_type, other_player_dismissed

Notes:
- "Runs" or "runs off bat" -> `runs_off_bat`.
- "Total runs" -> `runs_off_bat + extras`.
- Wickets are rows where `player_dismissed IS NOT NULL`.

**TABLE 2: public.rbi_cards_pos_atm_statistics_jun2025**
One row per bank, snapshot date 2025-06-30, 64 banks total.
All `_value_amt` fields are in absolute Rupees (NOT Rs '000). All `_nos` and `_volume_nos` fields are `bigint`.
Columns:
- Identity: category (e.g. Public Sector Banks, Private Sector Banks, Foreign Banks, Payment Banks, Small Finance Banks), date, bank_name
- Infrastructure counts: atm_crm_onsite_nos, atm_crm_offsite_nos, pos_nos, micro_atm_nos, bharat_qr_codes_nos, upi_qr_codes_nos
- Card base: credit_cards_nos, debit_cards_nos
- Credit card txns (volume + amount pairs):
  - credit_card_pos_txn_volume_nos, credit_card_pos_txn_value_amt
  - credit_card_ecom_volume_nos, credit_card_ecom_value_amt
  - credit_card_others_volume_nos, credit_card_others_value_amt
  - credit_card_cash_withdrawal_atm_volume_nos, credit_card_cash_withdrawal_atm_value_amt
- Debit card txns (same pattern):
  - debit_card_pos_txn_volume_nos, debit_card_pos_txn_value_amt
  - debit_card_ecom_volume_nos, debit_card_ecom_value_amt
  - debit_card_others_volume_nos, debit_card_others_value_amt
  - debit_card_cash_withdrawal_atm_volume_nos, debit_card_cash_withdrawal_atm_value_amt
- Cash at POS: cash_withdrawal_pos_volume_nos, cash_withdrawal_pos_value_amt

Presentation tip: for large Rupee amounts, the user usually wants Crore (divide by 1e7) or Lakh (divide by 1e5). State the unit in the column header.
